"cookie theft picture description" for a period of five years. 
The recordings were transcribed manually for research purposes. 
I have attached these transcripts, and will share the folder of audio files 
once we need it because of its size "around 4.5 GB". 

Please note that the not-preprocessed data has more 
dataset for different tasks. Please see them as they might be helpful to our model.
